The Parts of SD-WAN Deployment

Your Silver Peak SD-WANWide Area Network is made of three elements:

  • The Cloud PortalThe Silver Peak internet portal. All devices must be able to connect to the Cloud Portal to take advantage of Silver Peak features. - manages all licenses without user action
  • OrchestratorFormerly GMS. Silver Peak global management software. Manages, provisions, and monitors Silver Peak devices within a network. - must connect to Cloud PortalThe Silver Peak internet portal. All devices must be able to connect to the Cloud Portal to take advantage of Silver Peak features.; management and configuration software for Silver Peak EdgeConnect devices and NX/VX appliances
  • EdgeConnect - must connect to Cloud PortalThe Silver Peak internet portal. All devices must be able to connect to the Cloud Portal to take advantage of Silver Peak features.; creates network connections and moves data securely.

Figure 1. The Silver Peak Network

Your network will incorporate all three elements, so be sure to take this into consideration when designing your network.
