Troubleshooting Email

This topic describes ways to determine if your email is working properly.

Through Activity History

You can track the progress of an email created by a business object. For example, say you have an incident that generated an email through a workflow.

1. Open the Activity History tab to view the emails send and received.

Activity History Shows Emails Created

In this example, the last email in the list was generated by a workflow, but you can also track emails created within the incident record (see Using Email).

2. Select the email from the list, then click Go to. The Email window opens.
3. At the bottom of the window, click the Send Info tab. The send information comes up.

Email Send Info

The Sent value is false - meaning the email did not go through. The number of attempts are listed, and the errors are shown.
