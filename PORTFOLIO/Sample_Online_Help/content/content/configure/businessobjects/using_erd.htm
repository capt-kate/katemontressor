* * * New or Improved for 2014.2 * * *  Using ERD

You can view an Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) of some objects (ie. incident, change) from the ERD tab. This provides a high-level view of related objects.

The basic elements of the ERD are:

  • The object that stores information; in the center of the diagram.
  • Relationships between other objects; hover your mouse over a relationship line to view.

Sample ERD for the Address object

  • Click to open Legend at the bottom of the diagram, to show the symbol definitions.

ERD legend

  • Drag the related objects as needed.
  • Print out the diagram using the Print command for your browser.
