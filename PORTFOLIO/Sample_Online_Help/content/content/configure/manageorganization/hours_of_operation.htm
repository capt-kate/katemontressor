Setting Up Hours of Operation

The Business Hours page enables you to define the operational calendar for your company, including daily hours and schedules. For example, you can determine the response time for a technician to an incident or execute a business rule that escalates a call that has been open for a number of hours.

Business hours define how response times (such as those in escalations) are calculated. These response times are typically based on a specific hours of operation definition.

Add exemptions, such as holidays or days when the office is closed, to the calendar. You can have multiple lists, each with a unique name.

Add Recurring Exceptions

1. From the Business Hours page, click the name of a schedule.  The schedule page appears.
2. Click Recurrent to add a recurrence pattern. The Add Exception page appears.

Modify Hours of Operation
1. From the Business Hours page, select the name of the schedule you want to modify. The calendar interface appears.
2. Click Edit . The Edit Hours of Operation window appears.
3. Enter your information into the fields as described in Create an Hours of Operation Schedule.
4. Click Save. The hours are changed to the new settings.
