Supporting Organizations

 

Create a Supporting Organization
1. From the Configuration Console Settings pane, click Configure > Organization Profile > Supporting Organizations. The Supporting Organizations workspace appears.
2. Click New Supporting Organization. A blank form appears.
3. Enter a unique name for the new supporting organization.
4. Click Save. The new organization appears on the list.
Delete a Supporting Organization
1. Within the Supporting Organization workspace, select the organization that you want to remove.
2. Click Delete. The organization is no longer on the list.
