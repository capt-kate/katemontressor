Account Information

The Your Account wizard is part of the Application Setup Page.

Enter basic information about the tenant account. Here is where you set the defaults to use throughout HEAT, including, including your default time zone and currency. This information is also available under Settings > Configure > Organization Profile.

-information-
Field Description

What is the name of your company?

Type the name of the tenant organization.

Who is the primary contact?

Type the first and last name (in separate fields) of the primary contact person for this tenant account.

This is me

Select this option if you are the primary contact for this tenant account.

Phone

Type the phone number of the primary contact, if this person wants the phone number used.

Email

Type the email address of the primary contact, if this person wants their email address used.

What is the preferred time zone for outbound email for your organization?

Select the time zone format you want for outgoing email. This time zone is the default used in email actions related to the application, such as quick actions. See Timezones.

What is the preferred date/time format to display dates in outbound email?

Select the date/time format you want. This is the default format used in reports and dashboards.

What is the preferred currency symbol to display in the application?

Choose the currency symbol you want to use. You can set a single currency for the tenant. The selected currency appears in the Configuration Items Cost, Request Offerings, and Service Catalog workspaces. In other places, such as for the Cost field on the incident form, you can update the currency by entering the following expression: $(CurrentGlobalCurrency())

What is the primary location or headquarters of your company?

Type the primary street address of the tenant organization.

What are the standard hours of operation for this location?

Select the hours of operation for the primary location of the tenant organization. See Setting Up Hours of Operation.
Modify Account Information
  • From the Configuration Console Settings pane under Configure > Organization Profile, click Account Information.
  • Make your changes as needed, then click Save.
