Watch List

This feature must be set up by an administrator before it can be used. Watch List appears in the top tab bar area and is by default enabled for all roles except Self Service.

You can store frequently-used business objects in the watch list for quick access, much the same as saving bookmarks or favorites.

Watch List

The watch list automatically lists your most recently accessed business objects.

Watch List - Recent Items

Place an Object on Watch List
1. Within your workspace, select a business object. In this example, we chose Incidents.
2. From the toolbar, click Pin It.

Pin It Button

The object now appears under your watch list.
