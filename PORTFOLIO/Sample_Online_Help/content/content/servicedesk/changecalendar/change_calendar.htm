Using the Change Calendar

Changes, especially major and significant changes, can affect a large number of users in an organization. Organizations lose thousands of dollars and hours of work when employees are not notified or aware of impending changes that affect them. The HEAT change calendar provides greater visibility of impending changes in the organization.

The change calendar automatically picks up all scheduled changes and presents the information in a graphical view. The change calendar can be viewed by all the roles in HEAT.

Access the Change Calendar
  • Within the Service Desk Console, open the Change Calendar workspace.

Change Calendar Workspace

The default change calendar appears, showing the current month on the left and the current week in the main window.

  • Double-click an item on the calendar to open the change record.
Navigate the Change Calendar

At the top of the calendar are the following buttons:

Calendar Buttons

1. Close: Close the filters side panel.
2. Previous: Go to the previous day or month.
3. Next: Go to the next day or month.
4. Today: Go to today.

Update the Change Start and End Dates using the Change Scheduler
1. While logged in as Change Manager, open the change request record, then select the Change Schedule tab.

You can only update the start and end dates for the change if:

  • The schedule start date and schedule end date for the change is already filled in on the Change Details tab.
  • The change is already linked to a CI.
  • The change is not linked to a release. When a change is linked to a release, the start and end dates are filled in from the outage schedule of the release.
2. To update the change schedule:
  • Click and drag your mouse over the schedule to span a new date and time range

OR

  • Right-click and select Edit.
  • In the Edit Event window, enter a new start date and time in the Start at and End at fields.
  • Optionally, change the display text by entering it in the Title field.
  • Optionally, enter some details about the schedule in the Description field.
  • Click Save.

On the Details tab, the schedule start date and the schedule end date fields are updated with the new dates and times you entered.
