* * * New or Improved for 2013.2 * * *  Timezones

You can choose which timezones to show within HEAT and in what order the timezone appear in a list. All major world timezones have been included in HEAT, but you can create a timezone that meets your specific needs.

Using this option does not modify date and time information in the repository, but simply converts the date and time for the convenience of the user.

 

Create a Timezone
1. From the Configuration Console Settings pane under Configure > Organization Profile, click Timezones. The FRS_Timezone page appears.
2. Click New FRS_Timezone from the toolbar. The timezone configuration window appears.

Timezone Configuration Window

3. Enter your information into the fields.
-fields-
Field Description

Display Name

Enter the name of the timezone as will appear in a list. Click the Globe to enter translations of the name.

Timezone ID

Enter a unique name or number for the timezone.

Is Enabled

Select to enable the timezone.

Time Diff

Enter the number of minutes from UTC time. For example, the Nepal time zone is +5:45 hours from UTC, then you would enter +345. For San Jose, CA, the time is -7:00 hours from UTC, or -420.

Sort Order

Enter a number to designate in what order the timezone appears in a list.
4. Click Save. The new timezone appears in the list.

Each time you encounter a timezone field, the timezones are listed in the order you have defined.

Enable a Timezone

You can enable an existing timezone.

1. Within the Timezones workspace, select the timezone you want to appear.
2. From the toolbar, click Toggle Timezone State.

The timezone toggles between Yes and No under Is Enabled.

When you disable a timezone, you lose the sort order.
Delete a Timezone

You can delete a timezone you created if it is not being used on a form. Deleting the timezone field without removing it from a form causes the form to produce unexpected results. We recommend you remove the control from all forms before deleting the timezone from the business object.

1. From the Configuration Console Settings pane under Build, click Business Objects. The business object workspace appears.
2. Open the business object that contains a timezone field (such as Incident).
3. From the toolbar, select Object Fields. The field list appears.
4. Find your timezone field, then click Delete at the end of the row. Fields with grayed Delete icons cannot be deleted.

The timezone is now removed.
