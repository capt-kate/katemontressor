Incident

An incident is an unplanned interruption or reduction in quality of an IT service, or a break in the standard operation of a service. The details of an incident and its complete history from registration to resolution are recorded in an incident record.

View Incident records
  • Within the Service Desk Console, open the Incidents workspace. A list of incidents appears.
  • Double-click a record to view details.
