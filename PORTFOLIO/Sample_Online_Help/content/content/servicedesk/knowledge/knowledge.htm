Knowledge

The primary role of knowledge is to improve the quality of decision making by ensuring that accurate, reliable and trustworthy information is available throughout the service lifecycle. Knowledge is a collection of articles describing solutions and answers to Service Desk questions.

 

View Knowledge Records

Knowledge Managers and administrators can view knowledge articles. All roles have access to knowledge, unless restricted by the administrator.

1. Within the Service Desk Console, open the Knowledge workspace. The list of knowledge records appears.
2. Double-click a record to view details.
