Using the Survey

When an incident is closed, the user is invited by email to participate in a survey. It is important for users to provide feedback to the Service Desk about their effectiveness. From the incident module, the SDA can log information for on-demand customer feedback by logging the feedback by creating an incident with the category of Feedback.

When you receive an email notification:

1. Click the survey link or copy the survey URL and paste it in your browser's URL field.
2. Enter your answers to the questions, then click Submit.

The survey response is stored with the incident record.
