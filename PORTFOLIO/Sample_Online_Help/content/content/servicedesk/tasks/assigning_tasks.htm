Assigning a Task

If the owner is not specified during task creation, a task can be assigned to a team. The team manager can then assign an owner to a task.

Tasks are usually assigned to a team other than the Service Desk (desktop support), except for tasks created through the email listener. The Service Desk is the initial point of contact for the customer or requester, but tasks are usually performed by technicians with more specialized skill sets.

1. Find tasks that are in logged status. Tasks in logged status have not been assigned to owners.
2. Double-click a task record to open.
To go to the parent form from a task record, click Open in Parent from the toolbar.
3. From the Owner menu, select an individual to work on the task.
Optionally, you can select another team from the Team menu and an Owner from the new team from the owner menu.
4. Click Save from the toolbar.

The task assignee is notified by email of the task assignment and the task status becomes assigned.
