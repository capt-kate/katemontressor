Using External Tasks

External tasks are tasks that are acted upon by outside organizations, usually with whom you have a contract for services. For example, if your business provides a Web Hosting service. While you might be able to provide some services internally (such as web development or email), you might have an agreement with an outside vendor to provide various levels of access for your customers (fast, medium, or normal, for example).

Defined external task costs can be tracked, while ad hoc task costs cannot.

Access the External Task Workspace
1. Log into the Service Desk console as Administrator, then open the External Task workspace. The list of external tasks appears.
2. To see details, do one of the following:
  • Double-click a task.
  • Click the vendor name link.
Create an External Task
1. From the External Task workspace, click New External Task. A blank form appears.
2. Enter information into the fields.
-fields-
Field Description
Summary Subject or title of the task.
Details Details of the task.
Job Item This field is automatically generated by the system.
Unit Price Enter a price for each occurrence of the task.
Unit Quantity Enter the number of time you want to charge for the task.
Total This field is automatically generated by the system, and is a product of Unit Price and Unit Quantity.

Status

 The default status of Logged is generated when the task is first savedl
Priority Choose from the drop-down list: 1 (lowest priority) to 5 (highest priority).
Service Choose from the drop-down list.
Vendor The list in this field depends on the selection in the Service field. Choose from the drop-down list.
Contact Name Enter the name of the vendor contact.
Email Enter the email address of the vendor contact.
Phone Enter the phone number of the vendor contact.
UC This field appears if the selected vendor has a current Underpinning Contract. Choose from the drop-down list. If there is only one item on the list, it is automatically selected.
Task Catalog

This field appears if the selected vendor has a current Underpinning Contract for the Service. Choose from the drop-down list.
Vendor Case # Enter the Vendor's case number, if they have one.
Response Target This automatically appears for each case based on priority and urgency.
Resolution Target This automatically appears for each case based on priority and urgency.
Attach file Click to attach any images or documents related to this record. If this option does not appear, then you do not have privileges to attach files.
Comments Click Create, then choose a comment type (Email, Notes, or Voice Activity) to add comments to the record.
Team Choose a team from the drop down list.
Owner Choose an owner from the drop down list.
3. Click Save from the toolbar.
