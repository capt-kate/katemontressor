How to Log In

HEAT opens to the login page. Get the URL from your administrator.

  • Only users assigned to at least one role can log in, unless your administrator has enabled an anonymous login.
  • The user password is masked.
1. Enter your user name and password, then click Login.

Login Screen

The list of roles appears.

2. Select a role, then click Continue.

Select a Role Screen

  • You can only log in using one role at a time.
  • To change roles, log out, then log in again.
  • If you are assigned to only one role, you are automatically logged in as that role.

You are now logged in and ready to use HEAT.
