Search Not in English

Searches in languages other than English are based on the localized setting. For example, if your local machine is set to Japanese and the search criteria contains Japanese characters (for example, the title field), your search should be successful. If the field contains characters from multiple languages, only characters matching the local settings will be evaluated.
