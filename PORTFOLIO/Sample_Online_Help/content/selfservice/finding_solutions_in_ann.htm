Finding Solutions in Announcements

Highest priority announcements (as determined by the system administrator) appear in the Announcement workspace.

Find Announcements

1. Within the Service Desk Console, open the Announcement workspace. The system displays a list of announcements.
2. Enter keywords into the Search box. The system displays a list of announcements that contain the keywords that you entered.
3. Double-click an announcement from the list to view details.
