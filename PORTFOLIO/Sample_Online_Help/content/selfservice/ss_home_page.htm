The Self Service Home Page

The Self Service home page appears when you first log in. Self-help information is available from the following locations:

Search

Search for FAQs, announcements, and knowledge articles using keywords of the titles.
Announcements

Shows system messages for alerts such as downtime notices, service for maintenance or upgrades, potential virus threats, or other bulletin type messages that an organization needs to communicate to its employees.

My Open Items Shows a list of items currently assigned to you that are open. Click an item to see its details.
Report an Issue using Template This pane allows you to report an issue from a list of pre-configured templates. These templates automatically fill in certain fields and make reporting a little faster.
Top 5 Questions

Shows the top 5 most commonly-asked questions.

Top 5 Requests Shows the top 5 most common service requests.
My Open Items

Click an item from the list to view the item details. If your list is empty, you have no open items.

Find Solutions in Top 5 Questions

Click a relevant question or topic in the Top 5 Questions pane.

Top 5 Questions Pane

The FAQ tab appears, showing the details of the question you selected.

FAQ Tab

Report an Issue Using a Template
1. Click a relevant issue from the list in the pane.

List of Relevant Issues

The system opens the My Items tab where you can complete and submit the issue.

My Items Tab

The system displays the possible solution pane, which lists any relevant knowledge articles. Click a title to view the article.

2. Enter information about the item. Required information is indicated by a red asterisk (*).
3. Click Save Incident.
4. Click Back to My Items to see a list of your items.
Top 5 Requests
1. To create a service request from the Top 5 Requests list, click the title of an item. The system displays a blank form. Each form is different depending on the type of item chosen.
2. Enter information into the fields on each page of the form. Required information is indicated by a red asterisk (*).
3. When you are finished, click Review and Submit.  Review the information for accuracy.
4. If the information is not correct, click Editand modify the information. If the information is correct, click Submit.

After you submit a request, the system displays the Service Request creation complete window.
