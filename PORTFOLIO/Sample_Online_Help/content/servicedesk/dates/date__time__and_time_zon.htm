Date, Time, and Time Zones

You can specify which time zone options appear whenever you encounter a Date and Time control on a form.

The Date and Time controls reflect the local date and time of the user. The Timezone control enables the user to select a specific time according to a specified time zone, while indicating the local time of the user.

For example, users can choose a time and a time zone (including standard and daylight savings times) from a drop-down list:

Choose a Time and Time Zone

At the same time, their local time is automatically shown, so no conversions or mathematical calculations are needed to figure out time differences:

Local Time

Viewing Time Zones

To view a list of time zones, open the Time Zone workspace within the Service Desk Console.

Toggle Time Zone State

You can toggle a time zone between enabled and disabled from the Time Zone workspace.

Select a time zone from the list, then click Toggle Timezone State from the toolbar.

Select a Time Zone
