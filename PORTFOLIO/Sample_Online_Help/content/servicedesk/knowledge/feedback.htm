Working with Knowledge Feedback

The Feedback tab appears within a knowledge record and lets you see what feedback users have provided as well as enter your own feedback items.

Viewing Feedback Entries
1. Within the Service Desk Console, open the Knowledge workspace, then open a knowledge record.
2. Select the Feedback tab. The list of feedback items appears, if any.
Creating a Feedback Entry

Self Service users can provide feedback within the Self Service interface. See Providing Knowledge Feedback.

1. Within the Feedback tab, click New Article Feedback. The New Article Feedback window appears.
2. Enter information into the fields.
Field Description
User Rating The user rating. One star is not very useful, while five stars is extremely useful. Select from the drop-down list.
Is Article Useful Notes if the article was useful. This value is not related to the User Rating.
Comments Explanation of why you chose a specific User Rating.
3. Click Save. The window closes.

The feedback item appears on the list.

Modifying a Feedback Entry
1. Within the Feedback tab, click New Article Feedback. The New Article Feedback window appears.
2. Edit the information
3. Click Save. The window closes.
Deleting a Feedback Entry
1. Within the Feedback tab, select the feedback entry that you want to delete.
2. Click Delete .
