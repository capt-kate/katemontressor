Quick Actions

A quick actionA user-defined operation that performs one or more actions with a single click of the mouse. is a user-defined operation that enables you to perform a series of actions or advanced functions with a single click. For example, a quick action can copy an account file or send a confirmation notification upon receipt of an email. Quick actions are handy for often-used activities.

Quick actions are accessed by users logged into a specific role while in a specific workspace.

Your administrator defines which roles can create, edit, or delete quick actions. See System Permissions.
Viewing the Default Quick Actions for a Business Object
1. From the Configuration Console, click Build > Automation Tools > Quick Actions. The Quick Actions Manager page appears.
2. Next to the business object for which to see the quick actions, click Add/Edit. The quick actions for that business object appear under the Your Actions tab.
3. To view the details of a quick action, click the quick action name.
