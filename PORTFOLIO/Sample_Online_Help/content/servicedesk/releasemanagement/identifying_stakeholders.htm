Stakeholders

A stakeholderAn employee who might have a stake in the outcome of a record or approval. is an employee who might have a stake in the outcome of a record or approval. Stakeholders are commonly used for releases and budgets. Stakeholders are notified when they are linked to a record (via email) and work with the approval board to ensure that approved processes are implemented. Stakeholders might also have other functions, such as ensuring a back-out plan is in place or that the rollout into the live environment will have the least possible risk and negative impact, in the case of a release.

There is no approval process by default for stakeholders. They are merely notified when their employee record is linked to a record.

Adding a Stakeholder
1. Within the workspace for which to assign the stakeholder (such as Release or Sub-Budget Plan), choose the Stakeholder tab.
2. Click Link. A list of employee names appears.
3. Select the employee to link as a stakeholder and click Select. The employee is linked to the record as a stakeholder and appears in the list
4. Repeat to add more employees.
5. Click Save from the toolbar.

The employees you selected as stakeholders are notified by email.
