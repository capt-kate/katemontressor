Subscribing to a Service

Once a service has been published, each organization can subscribe to specific services. The easiest way to subscribe is to create and publish a service agreement.

The service types you can subscribe to are:

  • Operational Level Agreement. See Operational Level Agreements.
  • Service Level Agreement. See Service Level Agreements.
  • Underpinning Contract. See Underpinning Contracts.
