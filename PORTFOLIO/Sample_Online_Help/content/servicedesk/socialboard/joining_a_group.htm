Joining a Group

You can join any group that you can see.

1. Within the Social Board workspace, click Groups Activity > Manage Groups. The Manage Groups window appears.
2. If you have never joined a group, two buttons appear. Click Join a Group.
3. From the Public Groups tab, within the group box, click Join. Continue to join other groups as needed.

4. Click Close.
