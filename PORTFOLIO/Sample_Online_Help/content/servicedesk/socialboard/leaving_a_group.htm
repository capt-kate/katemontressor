Leaving a Group

You cannot delete a group, but you can leave a group. However, you cannot leave a group that you created.

1. Within the Social Board workspace, click Groups Activity > Manage Groups. The Manage Groups window appears with your current groups.

Manage Groups Window

2. Within the group to leave, click Leave. The Leave Group confirmation box appears.
3. Click Yes to leave the group.

The group no longer appears within the My Groups tab.
